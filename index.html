About Carson

I'm a Personal Fitness Trainer student at Mount Royal University with a passion for helping everyday people build a healthier relationship with fitness.

My journey into fitness training started with a simple belief: that exercise shouldn't feel like punishment. I've seen too many people burn out chasing extreme diets and unsustainable workout routines — and I wanted to offer something different.

Through my diploma program at Mount Royal University and my hands-on practicum experience at Gym MVT, I've developed a client-centred approach that meets people exactly where they are. Whether you're picking up a weight for the first time or coming back to fitness after a long break, I'll create a plan that actually fits your life.

I believe in honest conversation, genuine empathy, and practical guidance over quick fixes. My goal is to be the trainer who helps you build confidence in the gym — and carry that confidence into everything else you do.

Empathy First
I take the time to understand your story, your challenges, and your goals before anything else.
Evidence-Based
Every recommendation I make is rooted in current exercise science and best practices.
Long-Term Thinking
We're building habits for life — not sprinting toward a goal and falling apart after.
People-Centred
There's no one-size-fits-all solution. Every program is tailored to you as an individual.

04 — Practicum Experience

Hands-On Training

Practicum
Experience

MVT

Active Practicum Site

Gym MVT
Calgary, Alberta

I am currently completing my practicum placement at Gym MVT, gaining real-world experience working directly with clients under professional supervision. This placement allows me to apply the skills and knowledge from my diploma program in a real gym environment.

Initial Client Assessment
Conducting fitness assessments, reviewing health history, and establishing starting baselines with new clients to understand their needs, limitations, and goals.
Program Design & Delivery
Designing individualized exercise programs and delivering training sessions, adjusting plans based on client feedback, progress, and changing needs.
Technique Instruction
Coaching proper exercise form and technique, prioritizing safety and building client confidence in performing movements independently and correctly.
Progress Monitoring
Tracking client progress through regular reassessments and data tracking, celebrating wins and adjusting programming to keep clients moving forward.
